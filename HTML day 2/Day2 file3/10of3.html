Quartz is our most common mineral. Quartz is made of the two most abundant chemical element on Earth: oxygen and silicon. Atoms of oxygen and silicon kion together as tetrahedrons (three sided pyramids). These stack together to build crystals. Billions of tetrahedrons are needed to build even a small crystal.
